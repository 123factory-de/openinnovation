## Overview

100+ Accelerator is a global open innovation program backed by major consumer brands including AB InBev, The Coca-Cola Company, Colgate-Palmolive, Danone, Mondelēz International, and Unilever. It targets startups and scaleups with sustainability solutions and is currently active.

## Focus Areas

- Water stewardship
- Circular packaging
- Climate action
- Sustainable agriculture
- Biodiversity

## Benefits

- Pilot funding of up to $100K
- Mentorship and validation support
- Access to global supply chains and corporate partners

## How to Apply

- Apply through the official 100+ Accelerator website, which routes applicants to the program application flow. Review the live cycle details on the official site before submitting.
