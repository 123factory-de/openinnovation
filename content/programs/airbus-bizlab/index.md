## Overview

Airbus BizLab is a global aerospace accelerator that brings together startups and Airbus intrapreneurs to turn innovative ideas into viable businesses. The program supports projects across aerospace, mobility, and defense with acceleration support and proof-of-concept opportunities alongside Airbus experts.

## Focus Areas

- Aerospace
- Mobility
- Defense
- Acceleration and PoCs

## Benefits

- Acceleration program with Airbus mentors and experts
- Proof-of-concept projects with Airbus business units
- Access to Airbus technical and industry networks
- Support for both external startups and internal intrapreneurs

## How to Apply

- Apply through the official Airbus BizLab page when calls for startups and projects are open.
