## Overview

Allianz X is the digital investment unit of Allianz, investing in growth-stage companies that are shaping the ecosystems surrounding insurance and asset management. It focuses on insurtech, fintech, and healthtech, combining capital with access to the Allianz network and partnership opportunities.

## Focus Areas

- Insurtech
- Fintech
- Healthtech
- Digital Investments

## Benefits

- Growth-stage investment from Allianz X
- Partnership opportunities with Allianz business units
- Access to the global Allianz network and expertise
- Strategic support for scaling digital businesses
