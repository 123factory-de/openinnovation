## Overview

Heartfelt_ is an early-stage investment initiative from APX, the early-stage investor backed by Axel Springer and Porsche. It invites founders to pitch their ventures and gain access to funding, mentoring, and a broad network designed to accelerate companies from the earliest stages.

## Focus Areas

- Early-Stage Investment
- Pre-Seed Funding
- Founder Support
- Pitch and Mentoring

## Collaboration & Benefits

- Early-stage investment from a Berlin-based investor
- Hands-on mentoring and operational support for founders
- Access to the APX, Axel Springer, and Porsche networks
- Structured pitch process to connect with the investment team

## How to Apply

- Submit your pitch through the official Heartfelt_ page and follow the instructions provided for early-stage founders.
