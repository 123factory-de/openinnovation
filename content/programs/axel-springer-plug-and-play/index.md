## Overview

Axel Springer Plug and Play is an accelerator program run jointly by Axel Springer and Plug and Play. It connects ambitious startups with corporate partners and investors, with a particular focus on media and digital ventures, helping founders scale through industry access and mentorship.

## Focus Areas

- Digital and Media Tech
- Corporate Partnerships
- Investor Access
- Acceleration and Mentoring

## Benefits

- Direct connections to corporate partners for pilots and collaboration
- Access to investors within the Plug and Play network
- Mentorship from experienced operators and industry experts
- Exposure to a global innovation ecosystem

## How to Apply

- Review the accelerator details and apply through the official Plug and Play program page.
