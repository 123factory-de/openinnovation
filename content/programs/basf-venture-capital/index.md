## Overview

BASF Venture Capital is the corporate venture capital company of BASF, one of the world's leading chemical producers. It invests in startups and growth companies developing innovations in chemicals, advanced materials, sustainability, and digitalization. Beyond capital, it connects portfolio companies with BASF's technical expertise and global market access.

## Focus Areas

- Chemicals
- Advanced Materials
- Sustainability
- Digitalization

## Collaboration & Benefits

- Equity investment from a strategic corporate partner
- Access to BASF's research, technology, and global market reach
- Strategic collaboration with BASF business units

## How to Apply

- BASF Venture Capital typically invests after a proof of concept. Review the investment focus and contact details on the official BASF Venture Capital page to start a conversation.
