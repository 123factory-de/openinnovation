## Overview

BayStartUP is Bavaria's central startup network, connecting founders with investors, mentors, and partners. It runs business plan competitions and organizes financing rounds that help startups raise capital, alongside hands-on coaching for early- and growth-stage teams.

## Focus Areas

- Business Plan Competitions
- Startup Financing
- Investor Matching
- Coaching and Mentoring

## Benefits

- Access to one of Germany's largest investor networks
- Structured business plan competitions with expert feedback
- Coaching and individual support throughout the founding journey
- Connections to corporate partners and the Bavarian startup ecosystem

## How to Apply

- Explore current competitions, financing rounds, and coaching offers on the official BayStartUP page and follow the application instructions there.
