## Overview

The CUBIC Start Hub Program is part of Buhler Group's CUBIC innovation hub, created to foster collaboration with startups. It focuses on food processing, mobility, and advanced materials, enabling proof-of-concept projects that combine startup innovation with Buhler's industrial expertise.

## Focus Areas

- Food Processing
- Mobility
- Advanced Materials
- Startup Collaboration

## Benefits

- Proof-of-concept projects with Buhler business units
- Access to Buhler's industrial expertise and infrastructure
- Collaboration within the CUBIC innovation hub
- Opportunities to bring solutions to global markets

## How to Apply

- Learn more and explore collaboration opportunities through the official CUBIC Start Hub page on the Buhler Group site.
