## Overview

Colgate-Palmolive's portal accepts technical solutions and inventions from external innovators across oral care, home care, pet care, and packaging needs.

## Focus Areas

- Oral care
- Home care
- Pet care
- Packaging
- Technical solutions

## Benefits

- Portal review and screening
- Routing to the appropriate internal team
- Access to featured needs and strategic challenges

## How to Apply

- Choose a featured need or strategic challenge in the portal.
- Submit your solution through the official Colgate-Palmolive workflow.
