## Overview

The 3DEXPERIENCE Lab is Dassault Systemes' open innovation accelerator that supports disruptive startups working on hardware, deep tech, and sustainability projects. Selected ventures gain access to Dassault Systemes' 3D design and simulation platform, expert mentoring, and a global ecosystem. The lab helps transform bold ideas into tangible, impactful products.

## Focus Areas

- Deep Tech
- Hardware
- Sustainability
- 3D Design and Simulation

## Benefits

- Access to the 3DEXPERIENCE platform and design/simulation tools
- Mentoring from Dassault Systemes experts
- Connection to a global innovation ecosystem
- Support in bringing products from concept to market

## How to Apply

- Review the selection criteria and submission process on the official 3DEXPERIENCE Lab page and apply through the channel listed there.
