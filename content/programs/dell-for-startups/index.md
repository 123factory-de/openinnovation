## Overview

Dell for Startups is a program from Dell Technologies designed to help startups build and scale their technology infrastructure. Participants receive access to Dell technology, expert advisory support, and a network of partner benefits. The program supports startups across infrastructure, cloud, and data-intensive workloads.

## Focus Areas

- Infrastructure
- Cloud
- AI and Data
- Hardware

## Benefits

- Access to Dell technology and solutions
- Advisory and technical support
- Partner ecosystem benefits and resources
- Support for scaling infrastructure
