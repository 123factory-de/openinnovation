## Overview

The Dual-Use Drones Innovative Programme, organized by the European Cluster for Drone Innovation (ECDI) and co-funded by the European Union, aims to support small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) operating in the drone sector. The program focuses on helping companies technologically mature their drone solutions, structure their innovation strategies, and adapt their business models to meet civil, digital, social, and environmental needs.

## Focus Areas

- Technological maturation of drone solutions (upgrading technology readiness levels)
- Structuring innovation strategies
- Improving and adapting business models, processes, and products
- Developing or strengthening dual-use (civil and defense/security) applications

## Benefits

- **Equity-free funding**: Up to €35,000 per selected SME.
- **Mentorship & Assessment**: Individual assessment, roadmap definition, and ongoing mentorship throughout the program.
- **Tailored Services**: Access to technical, regulatory, business, and testing services.
- **Networking & Training**: Access to specialized training, internationalization support, and the possibility to join the Innovation Drone Alliance.

## How to Apply

Applications must be submitted electronically in English via the official [Wiin.io online platform](https://3742003280.wiin.io/en/applications/dual-use-drones-innovative-programme). Eligible applicants must be manufacturing SMEs operating in the drone sector with at least one drone product at TRL 6 or higher, committing to upgrade it by at least one level.

The application deadline is **September 30, 2026, at 5:00 PM CEST**.
