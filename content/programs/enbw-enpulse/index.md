## Overview

Enpulse is the innovation unit of EnBW, one of Germany's largest energy companies. It drives the energy transition by partnering with startups, building new ventures, and running pilots that bring fresh solutions into EnBW's business. Areas of interest span clean energy, e-mobility, and the digitalization of grids.

## Focus Areas

- Energy Transition
- E-Mobility
- Smart Grids
- Venture Building

## Benefits

- Access to EnBW's energy infrastructure, customers, and domain expertise
- Joint venture building and co-development of new business models
- Pilot and proof-of-concept opportunities with a major utility partner
