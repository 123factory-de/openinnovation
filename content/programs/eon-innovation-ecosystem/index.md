## Overview

The E.ON Innovation Ecosystem is a collection of programs through which the energy company E.ON collaborates with startups and partners. It includes GenAI Innovation, the E.ON Grid Startup Challenge, and the Free Electrons accelerator, spanning energy, grids, artificial intelligence, and decarbonization. The aim is to identify and scale solutions that advance the energy transition.

## Focus Areas

- Energy
- Smart Grids
- Artificial Intelligence
- Decarbonization

## Benefits

- Multiple entry points across dedicated programs and challenges
- Pilot and scaling opportunities with E.ON business units
- Access to E.ON's networks, infrastructure, and energy market expertise
