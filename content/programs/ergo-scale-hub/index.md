## Overview

The ERGO Scale Hub is the scaling program of ERGO, the primary insurance arm of Munich Re. It connects insurtech and digital startups with ERGO's business units to run proofs of concept and build commercial partnerships. The goal is to bring proven solutions into ERGO's insurance operations at scale.

## Focus Areas

- Insurtech
- Digital Insurance
- Customer Experience
- Process Automation

## Benefits

- Structured proof-of-concept process with ERGO business units
- Path to commercial partnership and scaling within ERGO
- Access to insurance domain expertise and a large customer base

- Review the program details and submission process on the official ERGO Scale Hub page and apply through the channels provided there.
