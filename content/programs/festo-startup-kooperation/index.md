## Overview

Festo Start-up Kooperation is the startup cooperation program of Festo, run within its research and development organization. It collaborates with startups on automation, robotics, bionics, and industrial technology through joint development and proof-of-concept projects. The program connects startups with Festo's engineering expertise and industrial automation know-how.

## Focus Areas

- Automation
- Robotics
- Bionics
- Industrial Technology
- Proof of Concept

## Collaboration & Benefits

- Joint development and proof-of-concept projects with Festo
- Access to Festo's automation, robotics, and bionics expertise
- Collaboration with Festo's research and development organization

## How to Apply

Startups can explore cooperation and joint-development opportunities through the official Festo Start-up Kooperation page.
