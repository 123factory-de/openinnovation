## Overview

FIEGE Ventures is the venture arm of the logistics provider FIEGE, which includes the F-LOG Ventures initiative. It invests in and collaborates with startups and scaleups in logistics and supply-chain technology. The unit connects portfolio companies with FIEGE's logistics operations and industry network to accelerate growth.

## Focus Areas

- Logistics
- Supply Chain Technology
- Strategic Investment
- Partnerships

## Benefits

- Investment and partnership opportunities with FIEGE and F-LOG Ventures
- Access to FIEGE's logistics operations and industry expertise
- Collaboration to scale logistics and supply-chain solutions

## How to Apply

- Startups and scaleups can learn more about investment and collaboration opportunities through the official FIEGE Ventures page.
