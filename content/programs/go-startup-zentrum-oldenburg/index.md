## Overview

GO! Start-up Zentrum Oldenburg is a regional startup and founder center operated by the TGO Technologie- und Gruenderzentrum Oldenburg. It supports founders with coaching, workspace, and practical founding support, helping new ventures get off the ground in the Oldenburg region.

## Focus Areas

- Founder Support
- Coaching and Advisory
- Workspace and Infrastructure
- Startup Community

## Collaboration & Benefits

- Coaching and advisory throughout the founding process
- Access to workspace and infrastructure for new ventures
- Connections to the regional startup community
- Practical support for early-stage founders

## How to Apply

- Find details on coaching, space, and founding support, and get in touch via the official center page.
