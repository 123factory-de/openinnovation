## Overview

Google for Startups Accelerator is an active accelerator platform for early-stage technology startups, especially AI-first companies. Multiple regional and thematic programs run in 2026, some with set application deadlines.

## Focus Areas

- AI
- Machine learning
- Energy transition
- ClimateTech
- FinTech

## Benefits

- Equity-free support
- Google expert mentoring
- Google Cloud credits
- Access to Google AI products and technical support

## How to Apply

- Apply through the official Google for Startups Accelerator portal and choose the relevant regional or thematic program. Review the specific live deadline for the track you plan to enter.
