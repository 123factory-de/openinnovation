## Overview

Google LaunchPad Accelerator is part of the Google for Developers accelerator programs. It provides startups with equity-free support, technical mentorship, and access to Google's products and engineering expertise.

## Focus Areas

- Artificial intelligence
- Machine learning
- Mobile development
- Cloud technology

## Benefits

- Equity-free participation
- Technical mentorship from Google engineers
- Access to Google products and tools
- Networking within the developer and startup community

## How to Apply

- Browse current accelerator programs and review eligibility on the official Google for Developers accelerators page, then apply to the relevant program.
