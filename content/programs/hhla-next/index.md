## Overview

HHLA NEXT is the venture-building unit of HHLA. It builds new ventures and partners with startups in maritime, logistics, and port technology. The unit combines HHLA's logistics and port operations expertise with entrepreneurial ventures to develop and scale new digital and technology-driven business models.

## Focus Areas

- Logistics
- Maritime Technology
- Port Technology
- Venture Building

## Benefits

- Venture building and partnership opportunities with HHLA
- Access to HHLA's logistics and port operations expertise
- Support in developing and scaling maritime and logistics ventures
