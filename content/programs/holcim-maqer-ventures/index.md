## Overview

Holcim MAQER Ventures is the open innovation and corporate venturing arm of Holcim. It partners with startups working on sustainable construction and building materials, running proofs of concept together with Holcim's business units to bring new technologies to market.

## Focus Areas

- Sustainable construction
- Building materials
- Construction tech
- Corporate venturing

## Benefits

- Proofs of concept with Holcim business units
- Access to Holcim's industry expertise and scale
- Pathways to commercial partnerships and pilots
- Support for scaling sustainable construction solutions

- Review collaboration opportunities on the official Holcim MAQER Ventures page and reach out from there.
