## Overview

hubraum is Deutsche Telekom's startup program for early-stage technology teams, especially across Europe and Israel. The official program model centers on startup calls, infrastructure access, and collaboration with Telekom business units.

## Focus Areas

- 5G
- AI
- IoT
- XR
- Magenta Business APIs

## Benefits

- Seed financing
- Mentoring and workspace access
- Access to Deutsche Telekom channels and infrastructure

## How to Apply

- Apply through the official hubraum site when relevant calls are open, or use the program's startup submission route where available.
