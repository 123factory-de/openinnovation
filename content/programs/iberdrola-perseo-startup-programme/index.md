## Overview

PERSEO Startup Programme is Iberdrola's startup collaboration vehicle for energy transition and digital innovation themes. The report describes it as active, with continuous challenge activity through the official NewTech Challenges structure.

## Focus Areas

- Renewable energy
- Grid resilience
- Decarbonization
- Digitalization and AI
- Energy efficiency

## Collaboration & Benefits

- Pilot projects
- Access to infrastructure and data
- Technical expertise
- Potential venture investment

## How to Apply

Submit proposals through the official PERSEO or NewTech Challenges routes on Iberdrola's program page and verify the active challenge relevant to your solution.
