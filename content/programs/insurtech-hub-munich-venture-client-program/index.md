## Overview

The Venture Client Program at InsurTech Hub Munich connects insurance and finance startups with established corporates through the venture client model. Instead of equity, corporates become early customers, buying and piloting startup solutions. This gives startups paid pilots and real-world validation while corporates gain fast access to innovation.
