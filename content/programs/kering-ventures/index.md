## Overview

Kering Ventures is the venture investment arm of luxury group Kering. It backs innovative companies advancing sustainability, material innovation, and retail technology relevant to the luxury and fashion industries. Portfolio companies gain capital alongside access to Kering's expertise and network of luxury houses.

## Focus Areas

- Sustainability
- Material Innovation
- Retail Technology
- Luxury and Fashion

## Benefits

- Venture investment from Kering
- Access to Kering's luxury and fashion expertise
- Collaboration opportunities with Kering's houses
- Strategic guidance and network access
