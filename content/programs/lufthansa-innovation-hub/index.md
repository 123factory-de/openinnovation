## Overview

Lufthansa Innovation Hub is the digital innovation unit of Deutsche Lufthansa, dedicated to exploring and building the future of travel and mobility. It combines market intelligence, venture building, and strategic partnerships to bring new digital products and business models to the Lufthansa Group.

## Focus Areas

- Travel Technology
- Mobility
- Venture Building
- Digital Customer Experience

## Benefits

- Partnerships with travel-tech startups
- Access to Lufthansa Group market knowledge and reach
- Venture building and product development support
- Strategic collaboration with airline business units

- Reach out through the official Lufthansa Innovation Hub site to explore partnership and collaboration opportunities.
