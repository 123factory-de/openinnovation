## Overview

The Lufthansa Group Start-up Kit is a benefits program designed to support startups with their business travel needs. As part of the Lufthansa Group programs portfolio, it provides advantages and flexible travel conditions tailored to growing companies.

## Focus Areas

- Business Travel
- Startup Benefits
- Corporate Mobility

## Collaboration & Benefits

- Business travel advantages across Lufthansa Group airlines
- Flexible conditions suited to startup needs
- Access to Lufthansa Group corporate programs

## How to Apply

- Register through the official Lufthansa Group Start-up Kit page to access the program and its travel benefits.
