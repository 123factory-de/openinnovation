## Overview

NetApp Excellerator is NetApp's B2B startup accelerator program supporting early-stage companies in data management, cloud, and deep tech. Selected startups receive mentoring, technical guidance, and the opportunity to run proof-of-concept projects with NetApp. The program helps startups validate solutions and scale through NetApp's ecosystem.

## Focus Areas

- Data Management
- Cloud
- Deep Tech
- AI and Machine Learning

## Collaboration & Benefits

- Mentoring from NetApp leaders and experts
- Proof-of-concept opportunities with NetApp
- Technical and business support
- Access to NetApp's partner and customer ecosystem
