## Overview

NTT Startup Challenge is a global incubation and startup challenge run by NTT. It focuses on ICT, telecom, and digital innovation, inviting startups to collaborate with NTT group companies and accelerate solutions with global reach.

## Focus Areas

- ICT
- Telecom
- Digital Innovation
- Group Collaboration

## Collaboration & Benefits

- Collaboration with NTT group companies
- Incubation and acceleration support
- Access to NTT's global network and resources
- Opportunities to pilot and scale solutions

## How to Apply

Apply through the official NTT Startup Challenge site when the challenge is open.
