## Overview

Opella's open innovation portal is an active submission entry point for external innovators. Promising ideas are screened by yet2 and routed to Opella for follow-up.

## Focus Areas

- New products
- New technologies
- New advancements

## Benefits

- Submission review
- Screening by yet2 before routing to Opella
- Follow-up on promising ideas

## How to Apply

- Use the official submission form on the portal.
- The portal is currently live and accepting entries.
