## Overview

OTTO DOCK 6 is the venture and innovation arm of OTTO, one of Germany's largest e-commerce and retail companies. It builds new ventures and invests in startups focused on commerce and retail technology. Through DOCK 6, founders gain access to OTTO's retail expertise, reach, and ecosystem.
