## Overview

PepsiCo Labs is PepsiCo's startup collaboration program for pilot-ready technology companies. The official site indicates an active program model tied to business-unit needs rather than a single evergreen application cycle.

## Focus Areas

- Supply chain
- Retail
- Marketing
- Sustainability
- Data science

## Collaboration & Benefits

- Pilot collaboration with PepsiCo teams
- Operational scaling opportunities
- Funding and executive sponsorship

## How to Apply

Monitor the official PepsiCo Labs site for current calls or business-unit-driven intake routes and apply when a relevant challenge or collaboration path is open.
