## Overview

Pioneer Lab is the incubator and innovation lab of the Carl Remigius Fresenius Education Group. It supports founders building solutions in education and health education, providing coaching, resources, and access to the group's expertise. The lab helps early-stage teams turn ideas into viable ventures.

## Focus Areas

- EdTech
- Health Education
- Founder Support
- Coaching and Mentoring

## Collaboration & Benefits

- Coaching and mentoring for founders
- Access to resources and expertise
- Connections to the education group ecosystem
- Support in building and validating ventures
