## Overview

Porsche Ventures is the corporate venture capital arm of Porsche. It makes strategic investments in early-stage and growth-stage startups across mobility, automotive technology, sustainability, and deep tech. Portfolio companies gain access to capital and to Porsche's strategic and technical expertise.

## Focus Areas

- Mobility
- Automotive Technology
- Sustainability
- Deep Tech

## Benefits

- Strategic venture capital investment
- Access to Porsche's expertise and network
- Collaboration and pilot opportunities with Porsche
- Support for scaling and market entry

- Review investment focus and contact details on the official Porsche Ventures page and reach out through the channel provided there.
