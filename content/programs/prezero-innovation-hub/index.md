## Overview

The PreZero Innovation Hub is the open innovation arm of PreZero, the environmental services division of the Schwarz Group. It works with startups on circular-economy, recycling, and waste-management challenges, turning promising ideas into proofs of concept within PreZero's operations. The focus is on advancing sustainable material flows and reducing waste.

## Focus Areas

- Circular Economy
- Recycling Technology
- Waste Management
- Sustainability

## Benefits

- Proof-of-concept opportunities within PreZero's operational network
- Access to the scale and resources of the Schwarz Group ecosystem
- Domain expertise in environmental services and circular value chains

## How to Apply

- Review current challenges and the submission process on the official PreZero Innovation Hub page and apply through the channels provided there.
