## Overview

REHAU New Ventures is the corporate venturing and open innovation arm of REHAU. It partners with and invests in startups working on polymer and materials innovation, construction, and sustainability. The unit pursues collaborations and investments that complement REHAU's core businesses.

## Focus Areas

- Polymer and Materials
- Construction
- Sustainability
- Industrial Innovation

## Benefits

- Partnership and collaboration opportunities with REHAU
- Corporate venturing and investment
- Access to REHAU's industrial expertise and network
- Support for scaling and market access
