## Overview

Reliance JioGenNext is the startup accelerator program run by Reliance Jio. It supports early-stage founders building digital, mobile, and consumer technology products by connecting them with mentors, partners, and the large Jio user base across India.

## Focus Areas

- Digital consumer tech
- Mobile applications
- Artificial intelligence
- Enterprise solutions

## Benefits

- Hands-on mentorship from Jio and industry experts
- Access to the Indian market and the Jio ecosystem
- Opportunities for partnership and distribution at scale
- Guidance on product, growth, and go-to-market

## How to Apply

- Founders can review eligibility and submit their startups through the official JioGenNext page. Check the portal for current cohort details and application steps.
