## Overview

Startup Lounge - REWE is a collaboration program from REWE Group that connects startups with one of Germany's largest retailers. It focuses on grocery, retail, and e-commerce innovation, enabling startups to run pilots with REWE. The program is designed to turn promising ideas into real-world retail collaborations.

## Focus Areas

- Grocery Retail
- Retail Technology
- E-Commerce
- Pilots and Proofs of Concept

## Benefits

- Pilots and proofs of concept with REWE
- Access to REWE Group retail operations
- Collaboration with retail and innovation experts
- Path toward long-term partnership
