## Overview

Startup Lounge - REWE Sued is a collaboration program from REWE Sued that brings startups together with the regional retailer. It focuses on food, grocery, and retail innovation, giving startups the chance to test ideas in a real retail environment. The program emphasizes practical proofs of concept and in-store pilots.

## Focus Areas

- Food Innovation
- Retail Technology
- Grocery
- In-Store Proof of Concepts

## Benefits

- In-store pilots and proofs of concept
- Access to REWE Sued retail operations
- Collaboration with retail experts
- Path toward commercial partnership
