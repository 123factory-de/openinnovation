## Overview

Seven Accelerator is the early-stage arm of ProSiebenSat.1, operated through Commerce & Ventures. It backs young consumer and digital companies, combining capital with the reach of one of Europe's largest media networks. The program is built around the media-for-equity model, giving startups access to advertising reach in exchange for equity.

## Focus Areas

- Consumer Internet
- Digital Media
- E-Commerce
- Direct-to-Consumer Brands

## Benefits

- Media-for-equity investment
- Access to TV and digital advertising reach
- Brand-building and scaling support
- Network across the ProSiebenSat.1 ecosystem

- Learn more and reach out through the official Seven Accelerator page on the Commerce & Ventures site.
