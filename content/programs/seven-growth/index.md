## Overview

Seven Growth is the growth-stage investment arm of ProSiebenSat.1, operated through Commerce & Ventures. It supports established consumer and digital companies that are ready to scale, combining capital with the reach of the ProSiebenSat.1 media network. The focus is on accelerating expansion through media-driven demand.

## Focus Areas

- Consumer Brands
- Digital Businesses
- E-Commerce
- Scaling and Expansion

## Benefits

- Growth-stage investment
- Media power for scaling demand
- Brand and market expansion support
- Access to the ProSiebenSat.1 ecosystem
