## Overview

SevenVentures is the media investment arm of ProSiebenSat.1, operated through Commerce & Ventures. It partners with growth-stage consumer companies, providing TV and cross-media advertising reach in exchange for equity or revenue share. The model is designed to accelerate brand awareness and demand at scale.

## Focus Areas

- Consumer Brands
- Digital Media
- E-Commerce
- Direct-to-Consumer

## Collaboration & Benefits

- Media-for-equity and media-for-revenue investment
- Large-scale TV advertising reach
- Cross-media campaign support
- Access to the ProSiebenSat.1 network
