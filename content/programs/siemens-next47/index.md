## Overview

Next47 is an independent global venture firm backed by Siemens. It invests in growth-stage startups and scaleups building B2B and industrial technology, helping them scale with capital and access to enterprise customers and ecosystems.

- Access to enterprise customers and ecosystems
- Strategic support from an experienced venture team
- Connections to the Siemens network
