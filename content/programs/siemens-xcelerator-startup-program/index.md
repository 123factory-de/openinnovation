## Overview

The Siemens Xcelerator Startup Program brings startups into the Siemens Xcelerator open digital business platform and marketplace. It supports companies building software and IoT solutions for digital transformation, connecting them with Siemens and its ecosystem partners.

## Focus Areas

- Digital transformation
- Industrial software
- Internet of Things
- Ecosystem partnerships

## Benefits

- Access to the Siemens Xcelerator marketplace
- Connections with Siemens ecosystem partners
- Support for digital transformation projects
- Opportunities to reach enterprise customers

- Review the program and application details on the official Siemens Xcelerator startup solutions page.
