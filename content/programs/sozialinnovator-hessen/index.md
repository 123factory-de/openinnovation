## Overview

Sozialinnovator Hessen is a program run by the Social Entrepreneurship Netzwerk Deutschland (SEND) that supports social innovation and social entrepreneurship in the Hessen region. It strengthens impact-driven startups and connects them with the resources and networks needed to grow their social ventures.

## Focus Areas

- Social Innovation
- Social Entrepreneurship
- Impact Ventures
- Ecosystem Building

## Benefits

- Support tailored to impact-driven and mission-led startups
- Connections to the regional social entrepreneurship ecosystem
- Access to networks, knowledge, and partners in Hessen
- Strengthening of social innovation across the region

- Learn more about the program and how to get involved on the official SEND project page.
