## Overview

SpinLab - The HHL Accelerator is a Leipzig-based accelerator affiliated with the HHL Leipzig Graduate School of Management. It offers an equity-free program with mentoring and access to a broad corporate and investor network, supporting startups across a wide range of sectors.

## Focus Areas

- Equity-Free Acceleration
- Mentoring
- Corporate Network
- Investor Access

## Benefits

- Equity-free participation in the accelerator
- Mentoring from experienced entrepreneurs and experts
- Access to a strong corporate partner network
- Connections to investors and the Leipzig startup ecosystem
