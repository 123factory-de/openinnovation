## Overview

Startup Autobahn is a corporate open innovation platform founded by Mercedes-Benz with additional ecosystem partners. It is positioned as an active collaboration route for startups and scaleups seeking pilot and validation work with major industry players.

## Focus Areas

- Automotive technology
- Digital services
- Smart production
- Sustainability
- Software

## Benefits

- Rapid technology validation
- PoC collaboration with business units
- Exposure to corporate and ecosystem partners

## How to Apply

- Apply through the official Startup Autobahn platform and monitor live regional or cohort-specific calls when available.
