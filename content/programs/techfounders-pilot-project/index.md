## Overview

The TechFounders Pilot Project is a program by TechFounders that matches startups with corporate partners to run pilot projects and proofs of concept. It focuses on industrial and B2B technology, helping startups validate their solutions in real corporate settings and build lasting partnerships.

## Focus Areas

- Corporate Pilots
- Proof of Concept
- Industrial Tech
- B2B Innovation

## Benefits

- Matching with corporate partners for pilot projects
- Real-world proof-of-concept opportunities
- Validation of solutions in industrial and B2B contexts
- Pathway to long-term corporate partnerships
