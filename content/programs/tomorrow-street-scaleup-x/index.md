## Overview

Tomorrow Street (Scaleup X) is a Vodafone-linked scaleup collaboration program focused on later-stage technology companies. The program is positioned around commercial access, ecosystem scaling, and enterprise-grade partnership opportunities.

## Focus Areas

- IoT
- AI
- Cybersecurity
- Digital transformation
- Telecom technology

## Benefits

- Commercial trials
- Access to Vodafone's global ecosystem
- Scaling support and partner exposure

## How to Apply

- Use the official Tomorrow Street contact or application route to initiate participation in Scaleup X and confirm the current intake process on the live program site.
