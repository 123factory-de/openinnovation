## Overview

Toyota Open Labs is Toyota Motor Europe's startup collaboration platform for mobility, energy, and sustainability themes. The report re-verified it as an always-open route with rolling applications through the official site.

## Focus Areas

- BEV energy ecosystem
- Hydrogen ecosystem
- Carbon capture and reduction
- Circular economy
- Smart communities
- Mobility for all

## Collaboration & Benefits

- Business development support
- Pilot and PoC opportunities
- Access to Toyota's customer base and ecosystem

## How to Apply

Submit the official online application form on Toyota Open Labs and review the current challenge domains before applying.
