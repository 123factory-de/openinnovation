## Overview

The Thuringian Regional Innovation Program (TRIP) is run by the Foundation for Technology, Innovation and Research Thuringia. It strengthens the regional startup ecosystem by connecting founders, offering coaching, and providing structured acceleration. The program centers on a founder community that supports early-stage teams across industries.

## Focus Areas

- Regional Innovation
- Startup Acceleration
- Founder Community
- Coaching and Mentoring

## Benefits

- Access to a founder community and network
- Coaching and mentoring
- Structured acceleration support
- Connections to the Thuringian innovation ecosystem
