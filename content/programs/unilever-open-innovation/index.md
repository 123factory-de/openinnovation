## Overview

Unilever Open Innovation is an always-open collaboration route for external partners with relevant sustainability and product-development solutions. The official portal combines active technical needs with a broader submission path for new ideas.

## Focus Areas

- Planet and Society
- Packaging
- Sustainable ingredients
- Climate action

## Collaboration & Benefits

- Partnership and technical co-development opportunities
- Access to Unilever business needs and global scale pathways
- Opportunity to align solutions with listed innovation priorities

## How to Apply

Submit a proposal through Unilever's official "Innovate with us" portal and review any live technical needs relevant to your solution before applying.
