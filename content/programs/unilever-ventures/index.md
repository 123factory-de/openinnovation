## Overview

Unilever Ventures is the venture capital and private equity arm of Unilever. It invests in promising early-stage and growth companies, with a strong focus on beauty, personal care, and digital commerce. Portfolio companies benefit from capital as well as Unilever's expertise, scale, and global network.

## Focus Areas

- Beauty and Personal Care
- Digital Commerce
- Brands and Consumer
- Growth Investment

## Collaboration & Benefits

- Venture capital and private equity investment
- Access to Unilever's expertise and global scale
- Strategic guidance and partnership opportunities
- Connection to Unilever's brand and distribution network
