## Overview

UnternehmerTUM, affiliated with the Technical University of Munich, is Europe's largest center for entrepreneurship and innovation. It supports founders from idea to growth through incubation, venture development, and corporate innovation programs. The center brings together startups, established companies, and talent across many industries.

## Focus Areas

- Entrepreneurship
- Incubation
- Venture Support
- Corporate Innovation
- Deep Tech

## Benefits

- Incubation and venture development support
- Access to mentors, experts, and investors
- Corporate innovation partnerships
- Workspace, labs, and a broad ecosystem network
