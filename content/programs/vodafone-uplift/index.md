## Overview

Vodafone UPLIFT is a startup collaboration and accelerator program that connects innovative startups with Vodafone business units. It focuses on connectivity, IoT, and digital solutions, enabling proof-of-concept projects that can scale across Vodafone's customer base and infrastructure.

## Focus Areas

- Connectivity
- IoT
- Digital Solutions
- Proof of Concept

## Benefits

- Proof-of-concept projects with Vodafone business units
- Access to Vodafone's network, infrastructure, and customers
- Mentoring and acceleration support
- Opportunities for long-term commercial collaboration

- Apply and learn more through the official Vodafone UPLIFT page.
