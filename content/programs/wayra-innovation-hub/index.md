## Overview

The Wayra Innovation Hub is the open innovation and corporate venturing arm of Telefonica (O2 Telefonica). It helps connectivity and digital startups scale by acting as both a customer and an investor. Through Wayra, startups can run commercial pilots with Telefonica's business units and tap into its global telecom ecosystem.

## Focus Areas

- Connectivity
- Digital Services
- Corporate Venturing
- Scaling

## Collaboration & Benefits

- Telefonica as both client and potential investor
- Commercial pilots with Telefonica business units
- Access to a global telecom and partner network
