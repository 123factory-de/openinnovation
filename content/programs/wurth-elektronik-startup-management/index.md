## Overview

Wurth Elektronik Start-up Management is a dedicated support program from Wurth Elektronik for startups working with electronics and electronic components. It provides hardware development support and partnership opportunities to help young companies bring their products to market. The program connects startups with Wurth Elektronik's component portfolio and engineering expertise.

## Focus Areas

- Electronic Components
- Hardware Development
- Electronics
- Partnerships

## Benefits

- Hardware development support from Wurth Elektronik specialists
- Access to a broad portfolio of electronic components
- Partnership opportunities to support product development
