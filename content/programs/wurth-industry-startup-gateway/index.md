## Overview

Wurth Industry Start-up Gateway is a program from Wurth Industry that engages startups working on industrial, logistics, and C-parts solutions. It focuses on supply chain and procurement innovation, running proof-of-concept projects with promising startups. The program connects entrepreneurs with Wurth Industry's industrial supply expertise and customer base.

## Focus Areas

- Supply Chain
- Procurement Innovation
- Logistics
- Proof of Concept

## Benefits

- Joint proof-of-concept projects with Wurth Industry teams
- Access to industrial supply chain and procurement expertise
- Connection to Wurth Industry's customer and logistics network

## How to Apply

- Startups can explore collaboration and proof-of-concept opportunities through the official Wurth Industry Start-up Gateway page.
