## Overview

ZEISS Innovation Hubs are collaboration spaces, such as those in Dresden and at KIT, that co-locate startups and researchers alongside ZEISS teams. They are designed to foster deep-tech collaboration in optics, photonics, and semiconductor technology. The hubs bring together entrepreneurial talent, academic research, and ZEISS expertise in shared environments.

## Focus Areas

- Optics and Photonics
- Semiconductor Technology
- Deep Tech
- Research Collaboration

## Benefits

- Co-location with ZEISS teams and academic researchers
- Shared infrastructure and innovation spaces for deep-tech development
- Proximity to ZEISS expertise and the broader research ecosystem

## How to Apply

- Startups and researchers can explore collaboration and co-location opportunities through the official ZEISS Innovation Hubs page.
