## Overview

ZEISS Ventures is the corporate venture arm of ZEISS. It makes strategic investments in and builds partnerships with startups and scaleups working in optics, photonics, medical technology, semiconductor technology, and deep tech. The unit aims to connect promising companies with the technology expertise and market access of the wider ZEISS group.

## Focus Areas

- Optics and Photonics
- Medical Technology
- Semiconductor Technology
- Deep Tech

## Benefits

- Strategic investment from a leading optics and photonics group
- Access to ZEISS technology expertise, infrastructure, and market reach
- Long-term partnership opportunities with ZEISS business units
