# PitchBook Primary Industry Sector Taxonomy

> **Source**: [PitchBook Industry Descriptions (Updated Aug 2021)](https://www.scribd.com/document/587811824/PitchBook-Industry-Descriptions-Updated-Aug-2021) · [How to search using industries, verticals, and keywords — PitchBook](https://pitchbook.com/help/using-industries-verticals-keywords)

PitchBook's industry classification has **three levels**: **Industry Sector → Industry Group → Industry Code**. Every company is assigned one primary sector. This document covers the **7 Primary Industry Sectors** and their **Industry Groups**.

> **Industry Group ≠ Vertical.** Industry Groups are the second level of PitchBook's *primary industry* hierarchy. *Verticals* are a separate cross-sector overlay (see [`pitchbook-industry-verticals.md`](pitchbook-industry-verticals.md)).

---

## 1. Business Products and Services (B2B)

Companies whose primary customers are other businesses.

| Industry Group | Description |
|---|---|
| Commercial Products | Equipment, tools, and products sold to enterprises |
| Commercial Services | Professional, staffing, and other business services |
| Commercial Transportation | Freight, fleet, and logistics services |
| Other Business Products and Services | B2B activities not captured by the groups above |

---

## 2. Consumer Products and Services (B2C)

Companies whose primary customers are individual end consumers.

| Industry Group | Description |
|---|---|
| Apparel and Accessories | Clothing, footwear, accessories, and related goods |
| Consumer Durables | Long-lived consumer goods — appliances, furniture, electronics |
| Consumer Non-Durables | Packaged goods, food/beverage, personal care |
| Media | Publishing, advertising, entertainment, and content |
| Restaurants, Hotels and Leisure | Food service, hospitality, travel, and recreation |
| Retail | Physical and e-commerce retail storefronts |
| Services (Non-Financial) | Consumer-facing services outside financial services |
| Transportation | Passenger transport and consumer mobility |
| Other Consumer Products and Services | B2C activities not captured by the groups above |

---

## 3. Energy

Companies involved in energy production, infrastructure, and services.

| Industry Group | Description |
|---|---|
| Energy Equipment | Equipment and hardware for energy production and delivery |
| Energy Services | Oilfield services, engineering, and clean-energy services |
| Exploration, Production and Refining | Oil & gas extraction, LNG, and refinery operations |
| Utilities | Power generation, grid infrastructure, water utilities |
| Other Energy | Energy activities not captured by the groups above |

---

## 4. Financial Services

Companies operating in capital allocation, banking, and risk.

| Industry Group | Description |
|---|---|
| Capital Markets/Institutions | Investment banking, asset management, trading |
| Commercial Banks | Retail and corporate banking |
| Insurance | Underwriting, reinsurance, insurtech |
| Other Financial Services | Financial activities not captured by the groups above |

---

## 5. Healthcare

Companies focused on improving human health outcomes.

| Industry Group | Description |
|---|---|
| Healthcare Devices and Supplies | Medical instruments, implants, diagnostics hardware |
| Healthcare Services | Hospitals, clinics, home care, and care delivery |
| Healthcare Technology Systems | Digital health, EHR, health IT platforms |
| Pharmaceuticals and Biotechnology | Drug discovery, development, and biotech R&D |
| Other Healthcare | Healthcare activities not captured by the groups above |

---

## 6. Information Technology (IT)

Companies building software, hardware, and tech infrastructure.

| Industry Group | Description |
|---|---|
| Communications and Networking | Telecom infrastructure, connectivity, networking equipment |
| Computer Hardware | Servers, peripherals, computing devices |
| IT Services | Managed services, consulting, system integration |
| Semiconductors | Chips, processors, microelectronics |
| Software | SaaS, enterprise software, applications |
| Other Information Technology | IT activities not captured by the groups above |

---

## 7. Materials and Resources

Companies involved in physical material extraction, processing, and supply.

| Industry Group | Description |
|---|---|
| Agriculture | Crop production, agribusiness, food supply |
| Chemicals and Gases | Specialty chemicals, polymers, industrial gases |
| Construction (Nonwood) | Cement, glass, and nonwood building materials |
| Containers and Packaging | Packaging materials and container manufacturing |
| Forestry | Timber, pulp, paper |
| Metals, Minerals and Mining | Ore extraction, metal processing, mining services |
| Textiles | Fibers, fabrics, and textile manufacturing |
| Other Materials | Materials activities not captured by the groups above |
