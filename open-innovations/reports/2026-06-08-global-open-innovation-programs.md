### Connect + Develop - P&G

- Region: Global
- Country: United States
- Sector: Consumer goods
- Status: Always Open
- Deadline:
- Eligibility: Innovators, entrepreneurs, startups, inventors, patent holders, and business partners
- Focus areas: Product technologies; packaging; in-store; ecommerce; supply chain
- Benefits: Potential partnership with P&G business teams and access to global brand commercialization pathways
- Application path: Browse P&G needs and submit an innovation through the Connect + Develop portal
