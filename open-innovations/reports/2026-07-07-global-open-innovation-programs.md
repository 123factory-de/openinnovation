## Program Details

### Opella Open Innovation Submission Portal - Opella

- Region: Global
- Country: France
- Sector: Consumer health
- Status: Always Open
- Deadline:
- Eligibility: External innovators with new products, technologies, or advancements relevant to Opella's areas of interest
- Focus areas: New products; new technologies; new advancements
- Benefits: Submission review, screening by yet2, and follow-up routing to Opella for promising ideas
- Application path: Use the official submission form and complete the portal workflow
- Official source: https://openinnovation.opella.com/
- Notes: The live portal exposes "Submit Your Solution" and "My Submissions" links, which makes it a current active submission entry point.

### Colgate-Palmolive Open Innovation Submission Portal - Colgate-Palmolive

- Region: Global
- Country: United States
- Sector: Consumer goods
- Status: Always Open
- Deadline:
- Eligibility: External innovators with developed technical solutions and inventions
- Focus areas: Oral care; home care; pet care; packaging; problem-specific featured needs
- Benefits: Portal review, screening, and routing to the appropriate internal team
- Application path: Submit an idea through the Colgate-Palmolive portal and choose a featured need or strategic challenge
- Official source: https://colgatepalmolive.yet2.com/
- Notes: The portal explicitly says users can submit ideas to strategic challenges and lists multiple live featured needs.

### Information on submitting a technical solution - Unilever

- Region: Global
- Country: United Kingdom / Netherlands
- Sector: Consumer goods
- Status: Active
- Deadline:
- Eligibility: External parties with technical solutions for Unilever challenges
- Focus areas: Technical challenges; new solutions; product and packaging innovation
- Benefits: Pathway to Unilever's latest challenges and solution submission route
